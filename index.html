About Me

My name is Kimberly Monroe. I prefer to be called Kim. I grew up in a small town in North Carolina. After college and grad school, I lived in Maryland for about six years.

While living in Maryland, I had a job as a consultative sales rep in a foreign currency exchange company. That company promoted me and moved me to Los Angeles. I have lived in Los Angeles for the past 14 years, switching careers over the years.
